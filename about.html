Our Story

Crafting Coffee Culture in Bloemfontein

Where Passion Meets the Roast

☕ Royal Roastery Bloemfontein is a beloved local coffee brand known for its artisanal roasting and cozy café atmosphere. We specialize in sourcing green coffee beans from around the world and roasting them to highlight each bean’s unique flavor profile.

What We Do

Coffee Roasting

We bring out the crema, acidity, aroma, and flavor of every bean with carefully calibrated roasts.

Café Experience

Enjoy breakfast, lunch, pastries, and specialty drinks like pour-overs and flat whites in our serene cafés.

Retail & Takeaway

We offer retail packs of roasted beans and convenient takeaway—plus seasonal specials to love.

A New Chapter Begins

Royal Roastery recently transitioned into new hands. We're thrilled to welcome our new owner Danie Jacobs, who shares the same love for perfecting every brew and delighting every guest. With a fresh outlook, exciting flavor innovations, and heartfelt service, the journey continues stronger than ever.

“New face, new flavors, and a whole lot of coffee love! Let’s toast to new beginnings.”
